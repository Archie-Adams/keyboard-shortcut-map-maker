Introduction

This site is a tool to produce a visual image of keyboard shortcuts or application keybindings.

Below is an example of what the site may be used to create.

To start your own, click 'Create new set' from the header menu.

Keyboards and sections can be added using the buttons at the bottom of the page.

To change the text of any key simply click on the key, and enter the new text in the pop-up.

To change the colour of keys first select a colour from the palette at the bottom of the page.
This is then the selected colour, and any key which is subsequently pressed will change to this colour.
To change the selected colour, simply click on another colour key.
To deselect colours click the currently selected colours key.
Note: the currently selected colours key will have a pressed down effect.

Keyboards and sections can be re-ordered by clicking the ▽ and △ symbols next to their headers.

Keyboards and sections can be deleted by clicking the 🚮 symbol to their top right.

The headers for the keyboards, the sections, and the filename (at the top of the page), can all be edited by mousing over and clicking on them.
